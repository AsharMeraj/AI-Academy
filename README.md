## 🌐 Landing Page

This is the first thing you see when you hit the app — a clean, straightforward intro to what AI Academy does and why it's worth signing up. I kept it focused: one clear value proposition, a call to action, and enough context to get someone curious. No fluff, just a quick on-ramp to creating your first course.

## 🏠 Dashboard

Once you're in, the dashboard is your home base. It lists all the courses you've created, so you can jump back into any of them without hunting around. There's also a welcome banner for first-timers and a sidebar that stays with you across the whole app — including on mobile.

<img src="/public/assets/Dashboard.png" width="800"/>

---

## 📝 Course Outline Page

This is where the magic starts. You type in a topic, hit generate, and the app uses Google Gemini to build out a full course outline for you — chapters, structure, the whole thing. From here, the background jobs kick off automatically and start generating all your study materials without you having to wait around or trigger anything manually.

<img src="/public/assets/CourseOutline.png" width="800"/>

---

## 📚 Course Material

Once your course is ready, this is where you actually study. I built out four different study modes so you can approach the material however works best for you — detailed chapter notes for reading, flashcards for memorization, a Q&A section for self-quizzing, and a full quiz to really test yourself. Everything here was generated by the AI based on your original topic.

## 💳 Pricing Page

AI Academy has a free tier to get started, but if you want to go deeper — more courses, more content — there's an upgrade path here. Payments are handled through Stripe, so checkout, subscription management, and webhooks are all taken care of on the backend.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|---|---|
| Framework | Next.js 15 (App Router) |
| UI Library | React 19 |
| Styling | Tailwind CSS + PostCSS |
| Components | Shadcn UI |
| Authentication | Clerk |
| Background Jobs | Inngest |
| Database ORM | Drizzle ORM |
| AI Models | Google Gemini |
| Payments | Stripe |
| Deployment | Vercel |
